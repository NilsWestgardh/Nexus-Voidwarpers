Nexus: Voidwarpers combines the magic of generative AI with UGC to create a trading card game where players make, play, and earn money from custom cards.

It takes about 15 seconds to generate a card.

The Vision

Create, play, earn

Create custom cards, then submit them to be included in the official game. Approved cards are available to all players to play with, and can earn their creator money each time their card is sold.

Community-led TCG

Unlike other TCGs, the community and Nexus team will co-create the game, and both review new cards before they're officially included in the game.

Future card creation process

1. New cards starts out blank.

2. Write directly to start creating.

3. AI auto-completes text, card type, and color.

4. AI generate a name, artwork, and automatically balances the card.

5. Your Nexus card is created!

The Game

Nexus is a strategic TCG which should feel familiar to veteran most TCG players. The game blends fantasy and sci-fi and takes place in a simulated universe called Nexus. Players assume the role of Voidwarpers, who are powerful beings able to warp between planets. They command both entities across the universe and reality itself in their pursuit towards victory!

AI + UGC

Creating a custom card will be easy, using the generative AI-powered creation tool which will be baked into the game. Let the AI create a whole card, customize every part of your card yourself, or collaborate.

Powers

Powers are unique abilities which players can throughout a match. A power is paired with a deck. There are 10 powers, and each deck can only have one power at a time.

Warping

Never get resource-screwed again! In Nexus you can "Warp" once per game to turn a base source of one color(resource generating card), into another the base source of another color. I.e. Red to Blue.
